## Introduction

Core Web Vitals turn technical measurements into actionable product signals: LCP measures how quickly main content appears, INP captures interaction latency and CLS reflects visual stability.

## Lab vs Field

Measure both synthetic (lab) tests and RUM (field) data. Synthetic tests help debug regressions under controlled conditions; RUM shows the actual user impact across devices and networks.

## Prioritization

Focus on pages that drive traffic and revenue, set performance budgets and add automated checks in CI to prevent regressions.

## Practical interventions

- Optimize images: modern formats, responsive sizes and compression.
- Prioritize critical resources and reduce JS on the critical path.
- Apply strong caching strategies for static assets and appropriate revalidation.

## Product integration

Integrate Core Web Vitals into product dashboards and assign clear ownership between frontend, infra and product teams. Correlate metrics with conversion and retention to justify trade-offs.

## Conclusion

Treating performance as a product concern allows teams to balance speed, features and cost, and to deliver sustainable improvements over time.
